Steve Job's Significance in Computer Science

Steve Jobs was one of the founders of Apple, which would go on to revolutionize the tech industry by releasing several everyday products we use today. He also Pioneered the personal computer era in the 70s releasing the first successful PC, and we still use his invention in computer science class today. After returning to the CEO position at Apple in 1997, Jobs revitalized the company and industry with his marketing skills and more modern designs, releasing his new products like the iMac. His company would go on to yet again release never before seen products such as MacBooks, iPod, iPhones, and iPads.
